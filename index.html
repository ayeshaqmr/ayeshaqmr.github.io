01 About Me

I'm a Computer Science graduate from FAST NUCES, passionate about building software that genuinely helps people. My work spans AI/ML, mobile development, IoT, and frontend engineering.

At OGDC, I worked with large-scale Oracle SQL datasets and implemented AI-driven data visualizations for their medical management system. My Final Year Project, Speak Dine, uses Gemini AI to make food ordering accessible for visually impaired users.

Outside of code, I'm exploring generative AI tools, reading about human-computer interaction, and occasionally breaking things to understand them better.

I'm equally drawn to cybersecurity — holding certifications in Google IT Support and Cisco Cybersecurity — and to data science, where I enjoy extracting insights from messy datasets and building models that tell a story.
